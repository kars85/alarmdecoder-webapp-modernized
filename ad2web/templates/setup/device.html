AlarmDecoder Settings

Finally, we need to configure the AlarmDecoder itself. Most of these settings are fine when set to the default, but the Keypad Address needs to be configured specific to your card.

"SE" panels need to be set to address 31 but on other panels you are welcome to use any free keypad address you have after it has been enabled on your card. Check your card documentation, or call your alarm system provider, to add one for the AlarmDecoder to use.

For some Honeywell/Ademco panels, clicking the Get card Info button will provide you with your installer code and more. This only works on panels that support the AUI protocol. You will be notified if it is not supported when you click.
